How does this game work?

.

This game will test your knowledge of TWICE songs by making you guess what songs are being played, what song contains these lyrics, and what song has an MV with this exact frame!

From there, you should have to guess what song is given.

The 1st Round involves you guessing what song is played from 5 seconds of a song. The 2nd Round involves you guessing what song is referenced by the lyrics. The final round involves you guessing what MV is that screenshot from.

Start Round 1

Going Once, Going TWICE is a song-guessing game!

The point system is as follows:

Round 1: 2 pts each
Round 2: 3 pts each
Round 3: 5 pts each
A wrong answer: -1pt
Skip: -3pts
Hint: Only 1pt

All with 5 items per round.
